Understanding & Approach

You've built a design language with its own internal logic — a rule-based visual system for the era of intelligence. You're looking for artists who can internalize those rules and then push against them to discover what's possible.

That's exactly how I work. I thrive inside constraint systems — treating boundaries not as limitations but as the edges of a creative space worth exploring. My practice spans digital illustration, generative design, motion graphics, and visual systems thinking. I pick up unfamiliar tools quickly because I learn by making, not by reading manuals.

I'm local to the Bay Area and available to begin immediately. Below is a proposed scope for an initial engagement, structured to let us establish creative rapport quickly before scaling into larger production work.

Proposed Deliverables
Icons & Visual Components
A cohesive icon set and reusable visual components built within your design platform, following the rules and principles of your visual system.
Shapes, Patterns & Graphic Elements
