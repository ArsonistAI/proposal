Design and development of a playful, elegant, modern website for 3Koncept, a female‑led creative consultancy — with creative animation, your watercolor artwork and content, and a user‑friendly backend for blog and editable sections. Delivered within the next few weeks.

Understanding & Approach

The site will incorporate creative animation across loading screens, scrolling interactions, page transitions, and parallax effects, leveraging your supplied watercolor artwork and written content. I’ll interpret 3Koncept’s colours, story, and creative direction to deliver engaging, dynamic visuals that enhance user experience.

A user‑friendly backend will allow internal updates to blog posts and other editable sections. This project includes development support. Collaboration will encourage experimentation and thoughtful design — openness to your ideas alongside professional expertise — with potential for long‑term collaboration on future client projects.
